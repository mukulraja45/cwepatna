About SSBSS

SSBSS has started the design and is an innovative solar company founded in 2011, with a vision to bring scientific rigor to the design and installation of solar mounting structures, and to promote solar rooftop mounting with indigenous solutions for plethora of requirements. SSBSS is headquartered in Bengaluru, India.

We have a strong team for executing the works of Operation Team, Operations team as Project Coordinator, Project Manager, Assistant Manager, Site engineers, Site Supervisors and Helpers, Electricians to take up turn key projects.
